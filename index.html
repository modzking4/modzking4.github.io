Who Am I

I'm a tech enthusiast by heart and a software engineer by trade, I have a passion for mobile development (IOS and Android) and front-end web development.

Designing and create websites and applications from start to finish for the end user to publish and use the site for their needs or business needs.

Define

Speaking to the clients to get the general spec/ scope of wha they are after from their site or application for their use case.

Design

Going through the design stages making sure that the client is involved within the design stage, this way they have their ideas heard and developed with the clients approval of the design progress to the next stage can be achieved.

Build

With the design of the application or site approved by the client, the build can begin with dedicated teams to carry out the work needed for the project. With the build taking place the client can be sent demo videos of aspects of the site working til the final product is delivered upon the deadline.

Launch

The launch is really the easy part, as with the source code you can pick the hosting provider that you choose to host your website and pick the extras that you need, like file hosting and protection. The applications have to be launched on their respected stores to be approved for role out by Apple or Google playstore.
